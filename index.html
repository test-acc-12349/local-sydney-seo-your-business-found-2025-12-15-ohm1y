Built in Sydney, For Sydney

Local Sydney SEO was founded in 2015 within a small co-working space in Surry Hills, born from a frustration with generic, one-size-fits-all marketing agencies. We saw countless brilliant local businesses struggling to be heard above the noise of corporate giants. From day one, our goal was to level the playing field, utilizing data-driven strategies and a deep understanding of the local market to give small and medium enterprises the visibility they deserve.

Our mission is simple: to connect quality local providers with the neighbours who need them most. We believe in transparency, integrity, and results that you can actually see in your bottom line. Everything you need to know about our local SEO services.

While SEO is a long-term strategy, many of our clients start seeing initial improvements in Google Maps visibility within 30-60 days. Comprehensive ranking improvements typically mature around the 3-6 month mark, depending on the competitiveness of your industry and current website health.
We specialize in local businesses across Sydney. This includes trades (plumbers, electricians), professional services (lawyers, dental, medical), hospitality (cafes, restaurants), and retail. If your business relies on local customers, we have the strategies to help you grow.
Standard SEO focuses on ranking globally or nationally. Local SEO focuses on "near me" searches and ranking in specific geographic locations (e.g., "Cafe Surry Hills").
